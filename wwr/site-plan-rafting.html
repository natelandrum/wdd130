Whitewater Rafting Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

Build revenue for rafting business by comunicating confidence, safety, and enjoyment to potential rafting newbies and experienced rafters.

Audience

The target audience are people between the ages of 16 and 65 that love the outdoors. They may be avid rafters or newcomers.

1. Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on).
The target customers are new and experienced rafters between the ages of 16 and 65. They love outdoor activities.
2. What do they want?
They want to have fun but also want to know they'll be safe.
3. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
They expect to find information on the company, destinations, and price.
